# Professional Services Agreement - DRAFT v2

**Parties:** Albion Retail Holdings plc ("the Client") and Meridian Insight Ltd ("the Supplier")
**Status:** Revised draft, marked up by the Supplier. Returned 9 April 2026.
**Note:** This is draft v2. Compare against services-agreement-v1.md (the Client's first draft) to see what changed.

---

## 1. Services
The Supplier will provide customer-analytics and reporting services as described in Schedule 1, beginning on 1 May 2026.

## 2. Term
This Agreement runs for an initial term of twelve (12) months from the start date, and renews automatically for further twelve-month periods unless either party gives notice not to renew.

## 3. Fees
The Client will pay the Supplier a fee of **£9,500 per calendar month**, invoiced monthly in arrears.

## 4. Payment
The Client will pay each correctly submitted invoice within **45 days** of the invoice date.

## 5. Termination
Either party may terminate this Agreement for convenience on **one (1) month's** written notice.

## 6. Intellectual property
All intellectual property created by the Supplier in the course of providing the Services vests in the Client **on full payment of all sums due** under this Agreement.

## 7. Limitation of liability
The total liability of the Supplier under this Agreement, whether in contract, tort or otherwise, shall **not exceed the total fees paid by the Client in the twelve (12) months** preceding the claim.

## 8. Use of AI tools
The Supplier may use artificial-intelligence tools to deliver the Services, provided that it remains responsible for the output and that no Client confidential information is used to train any third-party model.

## 9. Confidentiality
Each party shall keep the other's confidential information confidential and use it only for the purposes of this Agreement.

## 10. Governing law
This Agreement is governed by the laws of England and Wales.
