# Professional Services Agreement - DRAFT v1

**Parties:** Albion Retail Holdings plc ("the Client") and Meridian Insight Ltd ("the Supplier")
**Status:** First draft, prepared by the Client. Circulated 2 April 2026.
**Note:** This is draft v1. Compare against services-agreement-v2.md (the Supplier's revised draft) to see what changed.

---

## 1. Services
The Supplier will provide customer-analytics and reporting services as described in Schedule 1, beginning on 1 May 2026.

## 2. Term
This Agreement runs for an initial term of twelve (12) months from the start date.

## 3. Fees
The Client will pay the Supplier a fee of **£8,000 per calendar month**, invoiced monthly in arrears.

## 4. Payment
The Client will pay each correctly submitted invoice within **30 days** of the invoice date.

## 5. Termination
Either party may terminate this Agreement for convenience on **three (3) months'** written notice.

## 6. Intellectual property
All intellectual property created by the Supplier in the course of providing the Services **vests in the Client on creation**.

## 7. Indemnity
The Supplier shall **indemnify the Client** against all losses, claims and costs arising from any breach of this Agreement by the Supplier or any infringement of third-party rights.

## 8. Confidentiality
Each party shall keep the other's confidential information confidential and use it only for the purposes of this Agreement.

## 9. Governing law
This Agreement is governed by the laws of England and Wales.
