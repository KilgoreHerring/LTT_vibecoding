What is vibe coding?
You describe what you want in plain English. The AI builds it. You say what's wrong. Repeat until it works.
That's it. No syntax, no setup, no coding required. You get real, working software on screen - not a slide, not a mock-up. The only skill that matters is thinking clearly about a problem and saying what you mean.

Who does what

Your job is the thinking. The facilitator's job is the typing.

Your job
  • Choose the problem worth solving
  • Describe what good looks like in plain English
  • React to what you see - say what's wrong and what to change
  • Decide when it's done enough to demo
The facilitator's job
  • Drive the keyboard and the AI
  • Turn your words into prompts the tool can act on
  • Keep things moving and unstick the group
  • Handle the technical bits so you don't have to
You never have to touch a keyboard unless you want to.

What makes a good idea

Real. Specific. Contained.

Real

Something you've actually run into, not a theory about how things could be better. Pick a problem you've felt.

Specific

"We waste time copying the same numbers between two systems" beats "things are slow". Narrow enough to build in an hour.

Contained

One focused tool that does one thing well, not a platform. Lean smaller than feels comfortable.

Start here: "What's the most annoying thing I do over and over that a computer should probably be doing?"
